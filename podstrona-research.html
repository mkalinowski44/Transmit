Research coordination objectives

1. To create a pan-European, interdisciplinary network for techno-economic analysis of CCUS;

2. To understand the current status of science in TEA approaches for CCUS;

3. To improve the quality of CCUS TEA and thereby enable more informed investment and R&D decision-making;

4. To ensure that the existing and developed research is easily accessible and widely used in research and industry; and

5. To establish of the future direction for development of CCUS TEA and development of collaborative research bids and papers.
